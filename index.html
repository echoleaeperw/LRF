Abstract

Generating safety-critical scenarios is crucial for evaluating autonomous driving systems. Existing rule-based methods often lack diversity, while data-driven methods struggle with controllability. We propose Learning from Risk (LFR), a novel framework that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) incorporating prior knowledge to guide the generation process.

Our approach demonstrates superior performance in creating realistic, high-risk traffic scenarios compared to state-of-the-art baselines.
